About Atlas Tower

Atlas Tower: A world-wide independent infrastructure owner with high value tower sites in the US and Africa

Atlas Tower develops and owns high quality tower sites in the Republic of South Africa. We offer our tenants an easy path to expand their networks and improve communication to the end user. We strive to be better & faster at the tower development process and place a focus on identifying locations that are technically correct and easy for tenant installation.

Atlas Tower South Africa focuses on a customer service experience that is second to none. We assist the carrier in designing the right equipment configuration at the right price. Our leasing process is fast, friendly & supportive. Our local management staff are highly experienced, with a combined 20 years of industry knowledge. Atlas will consistently offer the right infrastructure for the best wireless network in South Africa.

High Quality Assets

Our sites are high quality and structurally sound. Our sites are located to meet current network needs but designed to solve future capacity and backhaul challenges.

Accountability and Service

Combining small company accountability with great customer service process. Our goal is a fast, inexpensive, and painless co-location process, and in turn, a tenant mandate for quick development of more assets from Atlas Towers.

Superior Tower Assets

We build structurally superior tower assets, we utilize locations that are technically correct, and offer a customer service experience that is second to none. Although we always want our locations to be valuable, we never assume that it is enough. Our tenants customer service experience provides credibly and longevity, in an ever changing infrastructure industry.

Atlas Tower Site Solutions

Easy Leasing

Leasing space for your wireless telecommunications needs couldn't be easier than working with VB. We have a dedicated team of quick, knowledgeable and approachable people ready to devote their time and efforts to make your experience with us effortless.

Team Approach

Your team consists of your main contact, a Regional Leasing Manager who will coordinate and set all terms with you; a Regional Site Manager who will review and approve your application and will work with you on issuing the NTP, and a Leasing Specialist who will provide all documentation to you.

Get It Done Quickly

Why is leasing space easy? VB understands how important it is for you to "get it done quickly, at a fair market rental rate and with an easy installation process" - and that is equally important to us! We provide this by ensuring:

• No application fees!
• Site documentation sent to you within 48 hours of application
• Site lease documentation is sent to you within 48 hours
• Lease document is executed by the owner first
